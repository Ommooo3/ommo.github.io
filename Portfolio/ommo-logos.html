Personal Branding: OMMO Logos

Listed as one of my more relaxed projects (further emphasised by the fact it was the "calm before the storm" of my upcoming final major college project of that year), these OMMO logos would hypothetically provide me with some identity if I went freelance. Simplistically designed in Illustrator, these colourful logos were fun to design due to their ability to be stacked and act as 'blocks'. Each letter is its own block and can be mixed and matched, though it's very limited due to the simplicity of my online name. I went through lots of different tests to get to the logos I have now, though I cannot find the sheet I drew on with the designs.
