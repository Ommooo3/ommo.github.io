About Me...


My path to becoming a Graphic Designer...

I've been interested in graphic design since the age of twelve following my artistic passion of drawing as a child. I loved drawing fictional characters, like Yoshi.

I continued my interest in graphic design following my Creative iMedia course at high school, which helped my chances of getting into college to pursue a two year long course in graphic design at Blackpool School of Arts.

By the end of the course, I was offered a placement in an apprenticeship supported by my college for an extra year, leading to where I am now.
